About

News is important for a number of reasons within a society. Mainly to inform the public about events that are around them and may affect them. The site was basically designed to deliver the news in an easier and faster way to anyone who has an access to the internet.

The website mission is to provide the global news immediately to people everywhere. Furthermore, the news is not targeted to a specific group of people so that the website has categorized the news to facilitate the process of reaching the information that is related to the user’s interest.

Traditionally, any news website is designed for the old-age (mostly over 20) group of people who specifically have an interest in tracking the news. However, this website has categories that might attract the attention of a younger age group like the teenagers

News from one country to another is important in today’s global economy. Knowing what is happening in other countries gives people a perspective of each other’s ways of life and cultural differences. The website will provide global news to everyone in a simpler, easier and faster way than the conventional way of reading the newspapers all in one place.

The website main purpose is to deliver news to the people so that it needs to gather information from different resources to fill its web pages with relevant content about entertainment, art, science, technology, sport and gaming. For the entertainment section, all its content will be gathered from fashion magazines and websites, movies trailer and the celebrities press while the science and technology section will get its content from the most known scientific magazines (national geographic series & nature) and it will provide statistics from the governmental and the educational organizations (for reliability). The technology web pages will also provide reviews and information for the all the new devices released by the pioneers in that field by gathering info about their products from their advertisements and conferences. Regarding the sports section, its content will be gathered from the matches’ reviews, the opinions of the experts and the results of the important championships and leagues. The last category is about gaming, the website will track everything published on the official websites of the designers and the producers of nowadays games and it will also provide reviews for the most famous games by the professional gamers from their blogs and videos.
